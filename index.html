Politeness Theory

Brown & Levinson (1987)

This site consists of instructional materials for Brown and Levinson's Politeness Theory. The materials are designed to help linguistic pragmatics students perform analyses of texts within this particular theoretical framework. The theory is divided into modules which each have a mini video lecture and an interactive quiz over the material.
